Red panda

Red panda is good friend with giant panda, because they have same favorite food, bamboo! They mainly live in the southwestern of China, and they also are endangered animals like giant pandas.

Amount

According to the survey, due to illegal hunting and worse environment, the total number of wild adult red pandas are less than 10000. Therefore, it is very important for government to protect the red pandas.

Feature

The feature of red pandas is totally different from giant pandas. Comparing to the giant pandas, red pandas have longer tail (28 – 59 cm) and smaller body (50 – 64 cm). Besides, they have red soft fur and more flexible movement.
